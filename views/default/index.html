Pre-Engineering Summer Program -- St. Mary's University

About

The Pre-Engineering Robotics and Java Programming Summer Program at St. Mary's University is designed to teach middle school and high school students to learn robotics, JAVA programming, and operations research. We will offer four two-week camps (for commuters only) in the summer of 2017 on St. Mary's campus. The camp time is from 8:30am to 4pm. The camp is free of charge for accepted applicants. A decision on the application will be made toward the end of April or early May 2017. Thanks a lot for your patience.
